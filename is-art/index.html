about this project

This is a recreation of Rhea Myer's Is Art (2014). Is Art demonstrates a simple interaction with a smart contract. By paying a small transaction fee, anyone can say whether or not this contract is itself a work of art, and the contract's status as "art" or "not art" is recorded permanently on the Ethereum blockchain.

This contract is deployed on Ethereum's Rinkeby test network, which is a non-Proof-of-Work test blockchain that is free to interact with.
